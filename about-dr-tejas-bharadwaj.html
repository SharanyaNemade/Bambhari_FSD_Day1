A Visionary in Ayurveda and Jyotishya

Dr. Tejas Bharadwaj B.A. is an accomplished Ayurvedic physician, a profound scholar of Jyotishya, and the driving force behind Ayurveda Ayatanam. With a unique blend of traditional wisdom and modern approaches, Dr. Bharadwaj has emerged as a leading figure in the world of Ayurveda, bringing the timeless teachings of ancient texts to life in the contemporary era.

Academic Background and Pursuits

Dr. Tejas Bharadwaj B.A. completed his Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS) from the prestigious Government Ayurveda Medical College, Bengaluru, in 2021. His journey in Ayurveda began with a deep-rooted passion for understanding the ancient healing systems that have guided human health for millennia. Currently, he is pursuing his MD in Roganidana (Diagnostics in Ayurveda) from the same institution, further honing his skills in diagnosing and treating diseases through Ayurveda’s profound principles.

Simultaneously, Dr. Tejas Bharadwaj is pursuing an MA in Samskruta from a renowned Sanskrit University, underscoring his commitment to mastering the language of ancient Ayurvedic texts. His dedication to both Ayurveda and Sanskrit enables him to interpret and apply knowledge from classical texts directly, ensuring the authenticity and purity of the treatments he offers.

Expertise in Ayurveda and Jyotishya

Dr. Bharadwaj’s expertise is not limited to the field of Ayurveda; he is also a recognized authority in Jyotishya (Vedic Astrology). His unique title of Jyotishya Vaidya reflects his mastery in merging astrology with Ayurvedic healing, an area of traditional medicine that emphasizes the influence of planetary forces on human health. His insights into planetary alignments and doshas (imbalances) have revolutionized the way he approaches diagnosis and treatment, allowing him to offer personalized care based on a patient's horoscope, or Kundali.

He is the creator of HoraAyurveda, the first and only AI-based platform that integrates Jyotishya and Ayurveda. HoraAyurveda predicts potential health concerns by analyzing a person’s Kundali and provides Ayurvedic remedies based on those findings, offering a futuristic yet deeply traditional approach to healthcare.

Founder of Ayurveda Ayatanam

Dr. Tejas Bharadwaj is the founder of Ayurveda Ayatanam—a sanctuary for those seeking authentic Ayurvedic healing. Under his guidance, the hospital has become a center for the holistic treatment of diseases, combining traditional therapies with modern diagnostic methods. Ayurveda Ayatanam has garnered a reputation for excellence in Panchakarma, Daivavyapashraya Chikitsa (spiritual healing), and a range of other specialized Ayurvedic treatments.

Scholarly Contributions and Publications

Dr. Tejas Bharadwaj B.A. is also a prolific author, with several critically acclaimed books to his name. His works reflect his vast knowledge of Ayurveda, as well as his deep understanding of the spiritual and metaphysical aspects of healing. Notable works include:

  • Ayurganapati: A treatise on Lord Ganesha’s connection to Ayurveda, exploring the healing aspects associated with the divine.
  • Ayurvishnu: A scholarly work connecting Lord Vishnu's various forms to the Ayurvedic science of health and longevity.
  • Ayurlalita: A book that delves into the influence of divine feminine energy on health and well-being.
  • Gruhamaushadham: A practical guide on household remedies that taps into the healing power of nature.
  • Shankara Ayurveda: Connecting the teachings of Adi Shankaracharya to the principles of Ayurveda.
  • Daivapathashraya: Focuses on spiritual treatments in Ayurveda and divine intervention in healing.
  • Vaidyanatheshvara the Ayurvedacharya: A guide on the role of Lord Shiva as the supreme Ayurvedic healer.
  • Shaariira Vaibhavam: A detailed exploration of physical and metaphysical anatomy.
