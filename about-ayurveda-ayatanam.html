Sacred Healing

Ayurveda Ayatanam is more than just a hospital—it is a sanctuary where one can experience the essence of Ayurveda, guided by ancient wisdom and divine knowledge. We revere Ayurveda as an embodiment of Lord Ganesha, whose sacred name in the Ganesha Sahasranama is "Ayurveda" (ॐ श्री आयुर्वेदाय नमः).

This reverence is at the heart of everything we do, as our goal is to explore and impart the profound knowledge (yatha taha jnyana) of Ayurveda as it is found in the Vedas, Puranas, and classical Ayurvedic texts (Samhitas).

Vision of Ayurveda Ayatanam

At Ayurveda Ayatanam, we strive to heal the body, mind, and spirit through the timeless principles of Ayurveda. Our hospital stands as a center for not only treatment but also education, where Ayurveda is studied in its purest form. We are dedicated to preserving and promoting Ayurveda as a complete system of healing, integrating modern advancements with ancient wisdom.

Mission of Ayurveda Ayatanam

The mission of Ayurveda Ayatanam is to uphold the integrity of Ayurveda while providing compassionate, effective healthcare. We serve not just as a hospital but as a center for learning and spiritual growth, helping people discover the true potential of Ayurveda. From treating diseases to spreading awareness, Ayurveda Ayatanam remains a beacon of hope and healing.

Meet Our Founder

The driving force behind Ayurveda Ayatanam is Dr. Tejas Bharadwaj B.A., an accomplished and respected Ayurvedic practitioner. He completed his BAMS at Government Ayurveda Medical College, Bengaluru, in 2021, and is currently pursuing his MD in Roganidana, along with an MA in Samskruta. With profound expertise in Ayurveda Samhitas, Vedas, Puranas, and Jyotishya, he has earned the prestigious title of Jyotishya Vaidya.

Dr. Bharadwaj is also an author of many influential books such as Ayurganapati, Ayurvishnu, Ayurlalita, Gruhamaushadham, Shankara Ayurveda, Daivapathashraya, Vaidyanatheshvara the Ayurvedacharya, and Shaariira Vaibhavam. His scholarly contributions have enriched the field of Ayurveda, and his creative passion led to the direction of Shankaracharya, the first-ever Kannada film on the life of this revered philosopher.

With deep devotion to his craft, Dr. Bharadwaj has also produced multiple educational video series, including Sundara Ayurveda (exploring the glory of Lord Hanuman in Ayurveda), Bhaskara Ayurveda, Skandayurveda, and more. These works reflect his passion for making the profound teachings of Ayurveda accessible to everyone.

Educating the Future of Ayurveda

Ayurveda Ayatanam is committed to advancing Ayurvedic knowledge and skills for students, professionals, and the public. Our organization regularly hosts educational courses, workshops, and camps that offer in-depth training on various aspects of Ayurveda. Our YouTube channel has been instrumental in reaching a wider audience, delivering valuable insights on Ayurvedic principles and practices.

Through these initiatives, we aim to cultivate a deep understanding of Ayurveda, empowering individuals with the knowledge to promote health and wellness in their lives and the lives of others.
